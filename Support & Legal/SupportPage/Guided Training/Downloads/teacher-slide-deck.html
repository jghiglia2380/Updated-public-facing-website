About PFL Academy

PFL Academy is a comprehensive financial literacy education platform designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to make informed financial decisions.

Our curriculum aligns with all 15 standards recommended by the Council for Economic Education and the Jump$tart Coalition for Personal Financial Literacy.

Through our innovative two-day learning model, students build both theoretical knowledge and practical skills they can apply immediately in their own lives.
