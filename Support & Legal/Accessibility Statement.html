Accessibility Statement

PFL Academy is committed to making its website and curriculum accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities.

Our Commitment

We strive to ensure that our platform is fully inclusive and usable by all learners and educators, regardless of ability. We aim to meet or exceed the standards defined by the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA.

Steps We Take

  • Responsive layouts and keyboard-navigable design
  • Alt text on all essential images
  • High contrast colors and legible font choices
  • Support for screen readers and assistive technologies
  • Captioned videos and accessible assessments

Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is a continuous process. We regularly review our site and applications for potential improvements and encourage feedback to help us identify and address accessibility barriers.

Third-Party Tools

While we strive to ensure full accessibility, some content or tools integrated into our platform (e.g., third-party simulations) may not yet meet our accessibility standards. We are actively working with vendors to improve compliance.
